Disaster Relief

Natural disasters leave thousands homeless and vulnerable. Mother Care Charity responds with immediate relief and long-term recovery plans to help communities rebuild. Disasters are sudden events that cause significant damage, disruption, or loss of life.

Emergency Aid

We distribute food, water, medicine, and temporary shelter to families affected by floods, earthquakes, and other disasters. Man-made disasters include industrial accidents, fires, chemical leaks, and conflicts.

Community Recovery

Charities play a crucial role in helping people and communities when disasters strike. Once the emergency phase has passed, we help rebuild homes and schools, support health systems, and provide counseling for survivors.

Medical Support

Our teams provide first-aid, emergency medical care, and essential medicines to injured individuals. Medical support prevents disease outbreaks and ensures continued treatment during and after a crisis.

Psychological Counseling

We offer counseling services to trauma-affected individuals, helping them cope with stress, fear, and emotional loss. Counseling helps survivors recover emotionally and rebuild their sense of safety.
