Healthcare Support

Good health is the foundation of a happy life. Mother Care Charity provides healthcare services, medical check-ups, and awareness programs for underprivileged families.

Medical Camps

Medical camps are temporary health clinics organised by charities, NGOs, or community groups to bring essential healthcare services directly to people who might otherwise struggle to access them. They provide free or low-cost consultations, basic treatments, vaccinations, maternal and child care, and health education.

Health Awareness

Health awareness helps people understand how their everyday choices, environment, and access to care affect their well-being — and encourages habits that prevent illness. Charities and health organisations run campaigns and workshops on nutrition, hygiene, vaccination, reproductive health, and managing chronic diseases.

Vaccination Drives

Vaccination drives are organised by charities and health departments to deliver vaccines to large groups of people, especially in areas where routine health services may not reach. They’re vital during outbreaks or when trying to protect communities from preventable diseases such as measles, polio, or influenza.

Mental Health Support

Mental health support focuses on helping people cope with stress, grief, trauma, or emotional challenges during difficult life circumstances. It includes counselling, peer support groups, and training community members to offer basic psychological first aid.
