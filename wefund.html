How Donations Are Used

50% Program Costs: Direct services — education, health, food and shelters.

20% Community Projects: Training, livelihoods and local partnerships.

20% Emergency Relief: Rapid response for disasters and urgent medical needs.

10% Admin & Fundraising: Operations, monitoring and transparent reporting.

We publish quarterly reports and receipts for every funded project. For detailed reports, email info@mothercare.org.
